Our Work

Aquase is a mineral water produced by PT. ERINDO MANDIRI. The company, located at Dharmawulan Street number 02, Prigen, Pasuruan, East Java, Indonesia, is a family company established in 1990. This company owns seven distribution and service points in East Java such as Surabaya, Gresik, Malang, Kediri, Jember, Sidoarjo, and Madiun. After a year, they developed the business by working together with agents in 30 cities all over Indonesia. In 1992, they started to export the products to various countries. Aquase is healthy because it comes from higher spring-water and it is processed hygienically. Because Aquase is a good-quality product, it is awarded with certificates like ISO 90001:2000, SNI, BPOM, Halal, and ASPADIN.
